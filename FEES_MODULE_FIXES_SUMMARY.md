**Files Changed:**
- `lib/repositories/fee_repository.dart` (Lines 412-421, 495, 532-542)

**Changes Made:**

  (s) => s.feeType.name.toLowerCase() == 'tuition fee',

## 🐛 Known Issues Remaining (Non-Critical)

### 1. Race Condition in Number Generation (Low Priority)
**Location:** `lib/repositories/payment_repository.dart`, `lib/repositories/invoice_repository.dart`

Receipt and invoice numbers are generated by reading, incrementing, and writing back the sequence. In a multi-user scenario, this could cause duplicate numbers.

**Recommended Fix:** Use UUID-based numbers or implement database-level sequence locking.

### 2. Concession Date Validation (Medium Priority)
**Location:** `lib/services/invoice_service.dart`

Concessions are not validated against the invoice month. Expired or future-dated concessions may be incorrectly applied.

**Recommended Fix:** Add date range check when calculating discounts.

### 3. Duplicate Invoice Detection (Medium Priority)
**Location:** `lib/services/invoice_service.dart`

Invoice duplicate detection only checks by month, not by fee types. A student could have multiple invoices for the same month with different fee types.

**Recommended Fix:** Add fee type combination to the duplicate check.
